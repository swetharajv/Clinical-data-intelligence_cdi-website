What we do

We turn messy lab data into usable files.

You focus on the science. We handle the data admin. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Duplicate removal

We identify and remove duplicate records across large datasets, preserving the correct version of each entry.

Test code standardisation

Inconsistent test codes, abbreviations, and naming conventions are reconciled into a single, consistent format.

Missing field reconciliation

We flag and address missing or incomplete fields, so your dataset is complete and ready for analysis.

Excel restructuring

Massive, unwieldy spreadsheets reorganised into clean, machine-readable formats your tools can actually process.

Format conversion

We deliver your clean data in whichever format you need — CSV, Excel, or structured for import into your system.

Fast turnaround

Most lab data processing jobs are completed within 48–72 hours, depending on dataset size. We'll confirm timelines upfront.
