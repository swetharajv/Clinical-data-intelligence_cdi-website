Safety First

Data Security Protocol

At Clinical Data Intelligence, safety is our primary focus. We have designed our workflow to eliminate the need for personal patient data.

Zero PII Processing

We explicitly never request or handle patient names, phone numbers, or emails. We only process anonymised fields: Patient ID, Treatment Code, and Date.

Strict Deletion Policy

All client data files are permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days of completing the service. We confirm deletion in writing upon request.
