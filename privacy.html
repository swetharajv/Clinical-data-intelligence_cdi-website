Privacy & Data Safety

Zero PII Policy

We explicitly never request or handle Patient Personally Identifiable Information (PII). We process only anonymised clinical exports containing Patient IDs and treatment codes.

Secure Infrastructure

Data is never downloaded to local laptops or physical hard drives. All processing occurs within secure, browser-based cloud environments with AES-256 encryption at rest.

All files are permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days of completing the service.

GDPR Compliance

Because we work exclusively with anonymised data as defined by the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), our processing does not trigger the standard risks associated with personal data breaches.
