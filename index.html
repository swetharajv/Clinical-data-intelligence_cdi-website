Common Questions

Frequently Asked.

What exactly is an anonymised CSV?

An anonymised CSV is simply a spreadsheet export from your practice management software with columns like patient ID number, treatment code, appointment date, and billing amount — but with all names, email addresses, phone numbers, and dates of birth removed beforehand. Most software like Pabau, Semble, Dentally, and Carebit let you choose which columns to include at export. We will send you exact step-by-step instructions for your specific software once you get in touch.

Is sharing this data with you GDPR compliant?

Yes. Because we only ever receive anonymised data with no patient names, contact details, or NHS numbers, the files we process do not constitute personal data under UK GDPR. This means there is no data processing agreement required and no risk of a patient data breach through CDI. You remain the data controller for your full records — we never see them. Full details are in our privacy policy.

How long does the free audit take?

We deliver your full Revenue Recovery Audit within 48 hours of receiving your anonymised CSV. You will get a clear report showing your dormant patient count, an estimated recoverable revenue figure, and a prioritised list of patient IDs flagged for follow-up — ready to hand directly to your reception team.

What happens to my data after the audit?

Your file is permanently and automatically deleted from our systems within 30 days of analysis completion — and typically much sooner. We do not retain, archive, resell, or share any data you send us. Our infrastructure uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS in transit. You can request deletion at any point by emailing us directly.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with CDI?

Not at all. If you can export a spreadsheet from your practice management software, that is all we need from you. We handle all of the data science, analysis, and formatting on our side. Your audit results are delivered in plain language — no jargon, no dashboards to learn, just a clear list of what to do next.

How long will the free audit remain free?

The initial Revenue Audit is free during our UK launch phase as we gather feedback and build our track record. We are being transparent: paid tiers will be introduced once the launch phase concludes. If you submit your data now, your audit will be completed free of charge — no retroactive billing, no hidden commitments.
