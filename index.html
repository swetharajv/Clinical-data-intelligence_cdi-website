The problem

What your booking software won’t show you.

Cliniko, Phorest, and Pabau show you totals. Revenue this month. Appointments this week. They don’t show you why those numbers look the way they do — or what’s quietly pulling them down.

Which practitioner is actually your most profitable?

Not by total revenue — by revenue per hour. A fully booked practitioner generating £50/hr costs you more than an empty slot. Most clinic owners have no idea this gap exists, let alone how large it is.

Which days and time slots are dragging your revenue down?

A diary that looks full often isn’t uniformly profitable. Certain slots consistently underperform not because they’re empty — because they’re filled with the wrong treatments. We find them and quantify the cost.

Which treatments have gone cold, and what’s it worth?

Treatments that were in your top five six months ago and have quietly disappeared. Packages sold but never completed. These are revenue gaps with a number attached — we calculate it and tell you what to do.

What we analyse

Six things your software tracks but never explains.

Every metric below exists in your booking software right now. The data is there. CDI is the layer that reads it, connects it, and tells you what it means.

Practitioner revenue per hour

Not total revenue. Per hour worked. The only number that tells you if your team is actually profitable or just busy.

Slot utilisation by day and time

Which days and hours are chronically underperforming in revenue, not just in bookings. The difference matters.

Treatment cold spots

Treatments that were in your top five six months ago and have quietly dropped off. Each one has a monthly revenue figure attached.

Package sell-through rate

What percentage of your sold treatment packages are actually being completed. Abandoned packages are pre-paid revenue sitting idle.

Client retention by practitioner

Some practitioners retain 80% of first-time clients. Some retain 40%. That gap compounds over 12 months into a significant revenue difference.

Revenue trend and forecasting

Month-over-month and week-over-week trends so you can see whether performance is genuinely improving or just seasonal noise.

Common questions

Frequently asked.

What booking software do you work with?

Any software that lets you export a CSV or connect via API. That includes Cliniko, Phorest, Pabau, Semble, Dentally, Carebit, and most other practice management systems. If you can export a spreadsheet from your software, we can work with it. We’ll confirm compatibility before we start.

Do you need access to our patient records?

No. We never see patient names, contact details, or medical information of any kind. We work with operational data only — booking records, treatment codes, revenue figures, appointment timing. This data does not constitute personal data under UK GDPR, so there is no compliance risk to you.

How is this different from the reports already inside my software?

Your booking software shows you totals and tables. It does not tell you which practitioner is generating the most revenue per hour, which treatments have gone cold and what that costs monthly, or which packages are being abandoned. And crucially, it doesn’t write you a sentence telling you what to do. That interpretation layer — the written intelligence memo — is what no software provides. That’s the CDI product.

How long does setup take?

Your dashboard is built and live within 48 hours of receiving your data export. The data export itself takes five to ten minutes on your end — we’ll walk you through exactly how to do it for your specific software. Your first intelligence memo arrives at the end of your first complete month.

Can I start with a free pilot before committing?

Yes. We’re currently offering a free pilot dashboard for one clinic in exchange for an honest testimonial if the service is useful. We build your dashboard using your actual data, you run it for a month, and you decide whether it’s worth continuing. Get in touch and we’ll talk through whether this is the right fit.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing depends on the size of your clinic and the complexity of your data setup. We give you a clear, fixed figure before we start — no hidden costs, no variable billing. Get in touch and we’ll come back to you with a specific number within one business day.
