The multi-site challenge

What gets harder when you scale to multiple locations.

Data lives in separate systems

Each clinic may run different booking software, export different formats, and track different fields. Comparing them is a manual spreadsheet exercise every month.

Practitioner performance is invisible at group level

You might know each clinic’s total revenue. You almost certainly don’t know which practitioner across all your sites generates the most per clinical hour.

No single view of group performance

Decisions get made on gut instinct and individual clinic reports, not on a consolidated view that shows which site is underperforming and why.
