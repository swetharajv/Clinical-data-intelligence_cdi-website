Tell us about your project

Who this is for

We work with students and researchers who need their data sorted.

University students

Undergraduate or postgraduate dissertations and thesis projects that involve a dataset you need to clean and analyse.

Clinical researchers

Running a clinical trial or observational study and need the data structured before you can run your analysis.

Independent researchers

Working outside of a university setting and need a clean, structured dataset ready to feed into your report or publication.

What we do

We help you get from raw data to ready-to-analyse.

Dataset cleaning

Duplicates removed, inconsistent entries corrected, missing values flagged, and formatting standardised — so your data is ready to use.

Data structuring

Disorganised spreadsheets reorganised into consistent, logical formats that work with SPSS, R, Python, or Excel analysis tools.

Basic descriptive analysis

Frequencies, averages, distributions, and summary tables — the foundational numbers your write-up needs, presented clearly.

Report-ready outputs

We deliver your cleaned data and any summary analysis in a format you can include directly in your dissertation, thesis, or paper.

What we don't do

We don't write your dissertation, draw your conclusions, or conduct advanced statistical modelling (regression, inferential statistics, etc.) for you. We help you get the data into a usable state so you can do the intellectual work yourself. If you're unsure whether your project fits, just get in touch and we'll tell you honestly.
